Event Planning Timeline

Timeline Action Items
6-12 Months Before • Determine event goals and objectives
• Set budget and secure funding
• Book venue at Marina Convention Center
• Form planning committee
4-6 Months Before • Finalize event agenda and schedule
• Book keynote speakers and presenters
• Arrange catering and menu selections
• Design marketing materials
2-3 Months Before • Launch registration and ticket sales
• Confirm AV and technology requirements
• Book hotel room blocks
• Order signage and branded materials
1 Month Before • Finalize floor plans and seating
• Confirm all vendor contracts
• Create attendee badges and materials
• Send reminder communications
1-2 Weeks Before • Provide final headcount to venue
• Conduct site walkthrough
• Prepare staff and volunteer schedules
• Test all technology and AV equipment
Day Before • Set up registration area
• Deliver materials to venue
• Brief all staff and volunteers
• Final venue walkthrough

Event Day Checklist

Setup & Logistics

Arrive early for venue access and setup
Set up registration desk with materials
Test all AV equipment and microphones
Place directional signage throughout venue
Verify catering setup and timing

Technology & AV

Test presentation equipment and screens
Verify WiFi connectivity and passwords
Check video conferencing setup (if hybrid event)
Test lighting and sound levels

Attendee Experience

Prepare welcome packets and badges
Stock refreshment stations
Brief registration staff on procedures
Verify coat check and parking arrangements

During Event

Monitor registration and attendance
Coordinate speaker transitions
Manage Q&A sessions
Address any issues promptly

Post-Event

Collect feedback from attendees
Retrieve all company materials
Thank speakers and vendors
Conduct debrief with planning team
