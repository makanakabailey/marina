6-Month Planning Timeline

6 Months Before

Set event date and book Marina Convention Center
Determine budget and guest count
Choose event theme and color scheme

4-5 Months Before

Send save-the-date notices
Book entertainment (DJ, band, or performers)
Select menu and catering options
Book photographer/videographer

2-3 Months Before

Send formal invitations
Order cake and desserts
Plan décor and centerpieces
Arrange party favors

1 Month Before

Finalize guest count with venue
Create seating chart
Confirm all vendor details
Plan event timeline and schedule

1-2 Weeks Before

Conduct venue walkthrough
Prepare welcome signs and displays
Create playlist or music selections
Finalize all payments
